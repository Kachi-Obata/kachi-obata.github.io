From inception, the International Centre for Nigerian Law has developed multiple expertise in delivering excellent legal services to a world-wide clientele.

Over this same period, the various law firms and persons who belong to the International Centre for Nigerian Law have found a need for collaboration in legal practice.

Out of this collaboration came the decision to have a consolidation of these law firms into one large full-service law firm.

The International Centre for Nigerian Law managed the merger process and our law firm is the product of that merger process. We are International Centre for Nigerian Law Legal Practitioners (ICFNL Legal Practitioners).

Our modern offices are extensively networked. By deploying our computer and communications technology, we are able to serve our clients in Nigeria and all over the world from our online offices in Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt. But, we do not give cyber service! We give friendly, prompt, and very efficient service.

Our practice is organized into six sections:

  • Arbitration and Litigation
  • Commercial Law
  • Corporate Services
  • Maritime and Admiralty
  • Extractive Industry
  • Technology

Each section is headed by a specialist who, in addition to being a member of the Bar, also has relevant qualifications and experience in the areas covered by that section.
